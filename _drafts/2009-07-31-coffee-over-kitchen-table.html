--- layout: post title: Coffee over the kitchen table date: '2009-07-31T01:57:00.002-04:00' author: B. T. Forsythe tags: modified_time: '2009-07-31T02:02:21.803-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2806030340477734957.post-7212812938872373449 blogger_orig_url: http://poetry.btforsythe.io/2009/07/coffee-over-kitchen-table.html --- As your Cheshire eyes laugh,
off-hand reading aloud, the other
entwined in languid cup-caress,
you pause for a nuzzling sip.

I feel the sibilant rush
of breath, watch rapture-flash
crease your brow, painted
in afterglow.

Shameless, you feed my thrall that sly, sudden wink.

Rapt and hungry, I envy those berries, flesh
shorn away, roasted, ground
between elegant fingertips, ritually
syncopated-dripwise sweated-steeped
in splendour, perfumed silken
powder-adorned
gods of your rising devotions, cooling
in the mid-morning sun.
