Spent Pages of a Journal

each page a day, a month, a week, biding
the rough cadence of its curator's conscious
breaths, the others wont to rome (roam?), n'e'r
(ne'er? no) ne'r paid in the same coin
of coins minted in dissimilar realms,
forged to tender delight
at premium left
only woe for the others
as they fester, grow,
learn bile and spittle,
the progenitors of lust, luck,
denial stamped officially
with God's boon upon these stones
of the realm fit to cover eyes,
passage between lips paid and purchase more.
"Make room!!!
Victim, hero, or martyr, tomorrow you will be known!"

B. T. Forsythe, 2009-03-04
