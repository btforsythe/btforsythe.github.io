Forsythe tags: - poem modified_time: '2009-07-29T13:24:21.948-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2806030340477734957.post-3611837084796832222 blogger_orig_url: http://poetry.btforsythe.io/2009/06/supplicant.html --- Spine rigid, my old friend, cold
abides the mean crowded shelf.

Jacketed dog-eared against dust,
he murmurs for want of slippers.
