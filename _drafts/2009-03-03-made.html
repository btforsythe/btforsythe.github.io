Made

Chew your boy slowly — it would NOT do
for him to stick in your throat.
Count!
You would do well to follow the prescribed count as you chew.
Do not fret if he's squeamish. These doubts will pass,
he was bred to this purpose.

We warn that you make sure his feet are clad softly.
If a pulsing cadence is experienced, this should not cause alarm;
his training does not leave him in the gullet — he places one foot after another yet!

Our guidelines will lead you to satisfaction
or we guarantee your money back.

B. T. Forsythe, 2008-07-05
