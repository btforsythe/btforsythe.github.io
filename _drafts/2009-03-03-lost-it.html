Lost it!

I saw a poem today.
Heard it first, actually.
Just so happened
it had managed to get stuck in my ear.

I could smell it dangling there,
wanting to be frankincense and myrrh
but really just sweat
and cheap incense.

Made me wonder
if it would be bitter or astringent.
Oughta give it a good lick.

So...

I made a grab,
but with a desparate twist,
it flipped its whimsy
and skittered across the floor,
cracked a window with its poignant wit
and ran free
(as well as it could, anyway
dragging so much angst).
