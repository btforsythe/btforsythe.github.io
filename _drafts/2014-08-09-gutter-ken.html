--- layout: post title: Gutter Ken date: '2014-08-09T18:17:00.002-04:00' author: B. T. Forsythe tags: modified_time: '2014-08-11T18:50:34.387-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2806030340477734957.post-1193138273339478634 blogger_orig_url: http://poetry.btforsythe.io/2014/08/gutter-ken.html --- We each lie in our gutter, little more than gutter
reaching to the street, we craft bodies from blacktop and rainwater,
lips from weeded anthills, ill-formed
we tap out a sidewalk patois, gutter to gutter speech.
