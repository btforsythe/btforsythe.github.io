Forsythe tags: - poem modified_time: '2010-07-09T09:25:42.125-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2806030340477734957.post-3736878275142578419 blogger_orig_url: http://poetry.btforsythe.io/2010/07/tool.html --- I have formed
      my head adamant
            to strike and drive the nail
home,
      my teeth to bite the face
            of the knurl,
      my arms to move mountains.

Nothing else.
