--- layout: post title: Simple Promises date: '2009-07-22T01:50:00.005-04:00' author: B. T. Forsythe tags: - poem modified_time: '2009-07-29T13:24:21.948-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2806030340477734957.post-7402456062758878285 blogger_orig_url: http://poetry.btforsythe.io/2009/07/simple-promises.html --- Promise me my ivory tower will remain
a bar of soap, slippery soaked
in tears of frustration
from shins knocked against the dashboard,
splinters picked from soft spots in fingertips, spurring
my tongue to simple, sharp, short words,
dictionary spurned.

Promise me I'll treat them as friends, strip paint with curses,
and never forget them.
