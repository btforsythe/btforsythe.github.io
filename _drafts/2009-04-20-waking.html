--- layout: post title: Waking date: '2009-04-20T16:50:00.001-04:00' author: B. T. Forsythe tags: - poem modified_time: '2009-07-29T13:24:21.948-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2806030340477734957.post-7663168734174119254 blogger_orig_url: http://poetry.btforsythe.io/2009/04/waking.html --- I wake, the warm half of a footprint
stomped into the underbelly of the kitten

who, meowling, rasps its tongue
across my eyelids,
along my grain from tip to root,
into my pungent furrows.

Disconsolate, it drags its belly across my stomach
to lie silently on my chest and grow cold.
